Brake Repair & Replacement

Safe, reliable brake service to keep you in control.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our brake services include pad/shoe replacement, rotor resurfacing/replacement, caliper service, and brake fluid flush. We diagnose issues like squeaking, grinding, or soft pedal and fix them right.

What's included

  • Restores stopping power
  • Eliminates noise and vibration
  • Extends brake component life
  • Safety inspection included

How it works

1

Diagnosis

We test brake performance, measure pad thickness, and inspect rotors, calipers, and lines.

2

Service

Worn parts are replaced with quality components. Rotors are machined or replaced as needed.

3

Test Drive

We road-test to ensure smooth, quiet operation and proper brake feel.

Common questions

How do I know if my brakes need service?

Squealing, grinding, a soft pedal, or longer stopping distances indicate it's time for a brake check.

How long does a brake job take?

Most brake repairs are completed the same day, usually within 2-3 hours.
