Our story

Your Trusted Auto Repair Partner in Dallas

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our community safe and moving forward.

Car Repair and Shell Service Center has been serving the Dallas community for over two decades. What started as a small gas station with a repair bay has grown into a full-service auto repair facility trusted by thousands of customers. Our founder believed in treating every customer like a neighbor, and that philosophy still guides us today.

Over the years, we've invested in the latest diagnostic equipment and continuous training for our technicians. We're proud to be a family-owned business that prioritizes integrity, transparency, and customer satisfaction above all else.
