Our story

Your Neighborhood Auto Repair Shop

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep Dallas drivers safe and mobile.

Car Repair and Shell Service Center has been serving the Dallas community for over a decade. Founded by a team of experienced mechanics who believed in transparency and craftsmanship, our shop has grown from a small two-bay garage into a full-service auto repair center. We pride ourselves on treating every customer like a neighbor, offering clear communication and fair pricing.

Our technicians are certified and passionate about cars. We invest in the latest diagnostic tools and ongoing training to handle everything from routine maintenance to complex repairs. Whether you drive a sedan, SUV, or truck, we have the expertise to get you back on the road safely.
